Delete Your Wassle Account

You can delete your Wassle account directly from the app. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Wassle app on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile section
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm that you want to delete your account
  5. An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your registered phone number
  6. Enter the OTP code to verify your identity
  7. Once verified, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including:
  • Profile information
  • Order history
  • Saved addresses
  • Payment methods
  • Location history
  • All other associated data
will be permanently removed from our systems.

Security Verification

For your security, we require OTP verification before deleting your account. The OTP is sent to your registered phone number to ensure that only you can delete your account.

Need Help? If you encounter any issues while trying to delete your account, or if you have questions about the deletion process, please contact our support team.
