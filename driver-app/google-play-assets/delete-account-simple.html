Delete Your Wassle Driver Account

You can delete your account directly from the Wassle Driver app. An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your registered phone number for verification. Once verified, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the Wassle Driver app
  2. Go to Profile section
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Enter the OTP sent to your phone
  5. Confirm deletion

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@wassleapp.com.
